**3. MVP PRODUCT DEFINITION: "PORTALO"**

**Positioning:** The link-in-bio tool that treats you like a business
owner, not a link list. Own your brand. Own your audience. Own your
data.

- Trust-first: Transparent billing, one-click cancel, no renewal traps,
  human support for paid users

- Ownership-first: Custom domain on free plan, full data export, you
  keep your URL if you leave

- Design-first: Every page looks distinct. No two Portalo pages should
  look alike

- SEO-first: Your domain, your meta tags, your analytics, your traffic

**4.3 Kill Criteria (When to Pivot or Shut Down)**

- KILL if: Waitlist conversion \<1% after \$500 ad spend (problem isn't
  resonant enough)

- KILL if: \<500 registered users after 12 weeks of launch (no
  distribution channel works)

- KILL if: Pro conversion \<2% at week 24 (free tier is "good enough,"
  monetization broken)

- KILL if: Monthly churn \>15% for 3 consecutive months (product isn't
  retaining)

- PIVOT if: Users love the product but won't pay --- explore
  transaction-based revenue model instead
